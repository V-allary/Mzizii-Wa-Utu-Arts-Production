About Us

MZIZI WA UTU ARTS PRODUCTION is a dynamic creative collective rooted in the celebration of African stories, identity, and humanity. We are storytellers, performers, and culture-makers dedicated to producing bold, thoughtful, and engaging artistic works that reflect the beauty and complexity of our communities. Through theatre, film, dance, music, and interdisciplinary performance, we strive to create experiences that resonate, challenge, and connect audiences across Kenya and beyond. At the heart of MZIZI WA UTU lies a deep belief in the power of the arts to uplift, inspire dialogue, and foster a shared sense of dignity and purpose. Our name — Mzizi wa Utu — embodies our commitment to human dignity and communal spirit, anchoring our work in the values that make us uniquely human.

Our Motives

Our mission is to champion culturally rich, socially relevant, and emotionally powerful art that honors African heritage, amplifies diverse voices, and ignites meaningful connection between artists and audiences. We are committed to creating high-quality original performances that explore stories of identity, resilience, and community; empowering emerging and established artists with platforms, training, and collaborative opportunities; engaging audiences through thought-provoking works that spark reflection and dialogue; and building bridges across cultures by sharing our art locally, regionally, and internationally. We believe that art is not only entertainment — it is a catalyst for empathy, transformation, and collective memory.

Becoming a Mzizi

Becoming a Mzizi means joining a creative family rooted in storytelling, growth, and impact. Whether you are an actor, writer, director, creative, or supporter of the arts, Mzizii Arts Production offers a space to learn, collaborate, and express your voice through theatre. Join us and be part of a community that believes in creativity, culture, and meaningful storytelling.

WHAT WE DO

Theater Productions

We write, produce, and stage original plays that tackle social themes, celebrate heritage, and invite audiences to experience the world through new perspectives.

Festivals & Events

We curate and participate in cultural events and festivals that celebrate artistic diversity and foster community engagement.

We craft cinematic and digital narratives that capture authentic voices and expand storytelling into film, web series, and multimedia formats.
